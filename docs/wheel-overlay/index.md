# WheelOverlay

WheelOverlay is a lightweight Windows overlay that shows the current position of your sim racing wheel's rotary encoder in real time. It displays your position labels — such as TC, MAP, or FUEL — in an always-on-top window so you can read your current mode at a glance without taking your eyes off the track.

WheelOverlay is designed for sim racers who use multi-position rotary encoders and want a visible, configurable display that stays on top of any racing simulator.
